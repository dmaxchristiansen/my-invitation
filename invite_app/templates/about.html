About MyInvitation

Beginning in early 2020, we at Faze II Productions assembled a team of experienced developers to pioneer new technology to the world of virtual invitations. Guiding us in this nobel endeavor was a question that proved to be paramount to our mission:

Why pay to create an virtual invitation when we can offer one for free?

And thus began what has been some of the most powerful and moving work we have every produced. Similar to the (albeit naïve) inception of Facebook, MyInvitation – by way of the innovative development team at Faze II Productions – strives to create a more socially just, verdant, and environmentally responsible world by connecting people via our free, user-friendly virtual invitation platform.

Like what you see at MyInvitation?

As an open source platform, we receive no revenue from our users other than the gratification from knowing that our exceptional attention to product detail allows the world to be a warmer, fuzzier place.
